Welcome to Manchester’s Bootstrap Business Club.

The Bootstrap Business Club is an informal monthly gathering for local techies, geeks and entrepreneurs who are setting up or running their own, self-funded online/tech business.

We meet monthly to share ideas, talk business, technology, marketing and anything else that comes up!

Next Event: Problem-Solving Surgery, Tuesday 10th July

This month Harold Sharples, founder of the charity “New Business Hatchery”, will be facilitating a (bootstrap) startup problem-solving surgery. Harold is an expert at creating innovation teams in order to create new businesses, strategies and products.
