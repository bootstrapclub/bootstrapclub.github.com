Welcome to Manchester’s Bootstrap Business Club.

The Bootstrap Business Club is an informal monthly gathering for local techies, geeks and entrepreneurs who are setting up or running their own, self-funded online/tech business and startups.

We meet monthly to share ideas, talk business, technology, marketing and anything else that comes up!

This Month

How Investors Think — Understand Them Before Trying to be Understood! By Ben Hookway

At TechHub Manchester, Tuesday 13th November, 7pm

Too many entrepreneurs seek funding from investors without understanding why they do the things they do. This talk discusses how angels and VC work, helping you understand their behaviour, and giving you a better chance of connecting with the right one.

Ben Hookway is an experienced start up founder and manager. Experienced in multiple start ups in the USA and UK, he has raised money from VCs for the last 10 years and even made them some money by exiting companies. He is now working on various project and is also co-founder of TechHub Manchester.
