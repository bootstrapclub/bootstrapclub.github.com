Welcome to Manchester’s Bootstrap Business Club.

The Bootstrap Business Club is an informal monthly gathering for local techies, geeks and entrepreneurs who are setting up or running their own, self-funded online/tech business and startups.

We meet monthly to share ideas, talk business, technology, marketing and anything else that comes up!

This Month

The New Year Detox!

At TechHub Manchester, 77 Dale Street, Tuesday 8th January, 7pm-9pm

This month is an opportunity to share ideas, recap on progress and get feedback from your fellow Bootstrappers. Or maybe you simply want to attend and help others with your constructive feedback.

We are kindly sponsored this month by Wayra, who are providing pizza and beers.

As always, all are welcome; whether you're a startup that's already launched or are still toying around with ideas.
