Welcome to Manchester's Bootstrap Business Club.

The Bootstrap Business Club is an informal monthly gathering of local techies, geeks and entrepreneurs who are setting up or running their own, self-funded online/tech business.

We meet monthly to share ideas, talk business, technology, marketing and anything else that comes up!

Next Session: Tuesday 8th May, The Madlab, Manchester

This month we shall be holding a series of open discussions on the following topics:

  • Testing a market — Identifying whether your business idea has legs and who your target audience is.
  • Getting an audience — Generating traffic and attracting the right prospects for your Bootstrapped business.
  • Staying afloat on the road to profit — The financial practicalities of bootstrapping a business.

We'll also have a couple of Show & Tells from local bootstrapped businesses. This month we have:
