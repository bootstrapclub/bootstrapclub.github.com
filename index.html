Welcome to Manchester's Bootstrap Business Club.

The Bootstrap Business Club is an informal monthly gathering of local techies and geeks who are in the process of setting up (or have already set up) their own, self-funded online/tech business.

We meet on the first Monday of each month to share ideas and talk business, technology, marketing and anything else that comes up!

All our welcome so feel free to turn up on the night. For more information, join the Bootstrap Business Club Google Group.
